Welcome to the QMB Scavenger Hunt!

This scavenger hunt takes you around the Queen Mother Building (QMB), home of Computing at the University of Dundee. Each clue reveals a letter and leads you to the next location. Scan the QR code at each stop to continue your journey.

The School of Computing at Dundee is a vibrant community, focusing on areas such as Human-Computer Interaction, Assistive Technology, Data Science, and more.

We also have an active student society, DUCS (Dundee University Computing Society), which runs events, coding competitions, and social activities throughout the year.

Good luck, and enjoy the hunt!
